These are our ideal jobs!

John - Cyber Security Analyst

Courtney - Cyber Security Penetration Tester

Ethiopia - Mobile App Designer

Sophie - Senior Project Management

Kim-  Information Security Advisor


When comparing our groups ideal jobs where are some similarities in a couple of our jobs. John and Courtney both have an interest in Cyber Security, John would be looking at ways that Cyber Security could be improved and Courtney as a penetration Tester would be looking at if she is able to penetrate the network and what can be changed to stop this in the future. Both of these jobs have a number of similarities that are closely related.

Kim also has some overlapping traits that John and Courtney both would be using in their job. As a information security advisor she would be also looking at how security can be improved in a business, so similar to what John would be doing.

Ethiopia’s ideal job is a little bit different to everybody else's, Ethiopia as an app designer would be responsible for designing apps on mobile for businesses. The only common skills that Ethiopia would have with John, Kim and Courtney would be identifying what the business needs when designing the application.

Sophie's ideal job is to be a Project engineer at a Oil & Gas Company, her job would be be as a lead engineer in a team that designs, procures and builds skids. This job would involve working with a range of customers and involve a broad range of applications. There really isnt too much overlap with the other members of the group.
